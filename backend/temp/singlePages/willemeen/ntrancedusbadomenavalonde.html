BACK ALLEY RITUAL 4: METALUCIFER returns to Europe for special Anniversary Tour COME EARLY & LEAVE LATE this is a EXCLUSIVE NETHERLANDS DATE!

METALUCIFER
Japanese cult Heavy Metal heroes METALUCIFER are coming back to Europe to celebrate 30th anniversary of the band. The band was originally formed in 1995 as a side project of Gezol, vocalist and bassist of the legendary Japanese black metal act SABBAT, but quickly turned into regular act with legions of loyal fans worldwide.

INTRANCED
Metalucifer will be joined on this tour by INTRANCED, young Heavy Metal band from the US (feat. ex members of WHITE WIZZARD and HOLY GRAIL), which recently debuted with the "Muerte Y Metal" longplay on High Roller Records.

BAD OMEN
Ancient Evil Rock Returns once more: this power trio comes straight from hell to crush your soul, break your neck and make you beg for the Devil!

AVALON
Power… Speed…. AVALON! True Heavy Metal Maniacs teleported straight from the 80’s! These Germans will drive you insane with their total demented sound!
