CICADA (us) Demented, schizophrenic and unsettling hardcore blitz attack.

Ever wonder what it feels like to listen to a nightmare? 9 tracks of crazed, unrelenting sonic power. Demented and distorted guitar rhythms screech over guttural vocal pulsations, driven by a haunting barrage of low end mayhem. The first vinyl offering from CICADA exposes the sound of a thousand bad dreams. A sinister yet brilliant manifestation of wicked minds…

NECRON 9 (US)

10 track vinyl debut. Desperate, urgent, frantic blasts of sonic lunacy driven by a world gone mad.

Excellent 1982/1983 sounding hardcore. It reminds me a little of Lansing, Michigan’s The Fix or Poison Idea‘s “Pick Your King” ep.

ANGSTMÄLER

New Dutch hardcore band from Haarlem with members of Traumatizer and Inside Job. Think Jesus and the Gospelfuckers & Public Acid lovechild. Total dbeat destruction.
