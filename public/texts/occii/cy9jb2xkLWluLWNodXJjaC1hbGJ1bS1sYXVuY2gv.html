Cold in Church + Smári Róbertsson + dj’s tba

Cold in Church serves twin fantasy-bordello in all their finery. Gear yourselves for longing, legs, and reverb-soaked nostalgia. Projecting onto you our visions of mythic pasts, we will indulge your Nanny Saviour complex with hairy melancholia. Within the walls of the convent strings are strung, and chords clung to with existential ardour.

The Cold in Church debut album launch will be kicked off with a set by Smári Róbertsson. Afterwards there will be b2b DJ sets yet to be announced.

Cold in Church is a dream pop band and performance collective whose sets have been staged most recently in OT301, during Rijksakademie Open 23′, and Nieuw Dakota.
