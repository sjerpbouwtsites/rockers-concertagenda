GREAT JOY goes bang-bang in spite of entropy.

Australian/Dutch wonk-punk band GREAT JOY creates a cathartic party experience for singing, dancing, releasing and reflecting. It is confrontation as a playful act – an opposition to what troubles us in a format that excites us. It is frustration and pain and vulnerability and love all rolled into one.

GREAT JOY is an evolving ritual in which we collectively embrace our inner wonk, ferociously reach for the divine and, above all, get loose. It’s a light-hearted rampage in celebration of art and the out crowd, and you are all invited.

Join us on April 19th for a night of erratic-melodic catharsis and introspective dance punk and release our 3rd EP into the world with us & more acts TBA.
