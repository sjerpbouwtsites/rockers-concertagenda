URBAN WASTE (NYC, USA)

One of the pioneers of New York Hardcore scene. formed in the Spring of 1981. After releasing a sole 7″ E.P. in 1982, the band mainly played in the north east until 1984, when they split up. In 2008, the band reformed, and in 2010 released “Recycled” 12″ album. Their latest releases are “What else is new?!” self-released LP and “NYHC document 1981-1983” LP on Puke N Vomit Records.

They’re coming back to Europe this Summer to play just four shows – so don’t miss it!
