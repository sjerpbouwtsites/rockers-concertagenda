KOLPEKA (Basque Country, hardcore/punk/oi!, Mendeku Diskak Records)
Early NYHC inspired hardcore/punk straight from Guernica, Basque Country.
Think of Straight Ahead, Rest In Pieces, The Abused and Cause For Alarm, and add an oi! touch.

INSTRUCTOR (Brussels hardcore, Quality Control Records)
Straight from the heart of downtown Brussels, come the Cobra Jaune Skins, delivering late 80s heavy hardcore. You’ll love them either you are into that Breakdown ’87 demo or Kickback‘s ‘Cornered’ record.

PRESSURE PACT (Holland’s finest)
Citing such influences as SSD, Celtic Frost, and Heresy, Pressure Pact is a Dutch hardcore band that came to fill Boston Strangler’s void.
Don’t sleep on this.
