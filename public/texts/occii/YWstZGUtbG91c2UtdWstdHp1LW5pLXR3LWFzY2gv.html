|| BRAK (DE)
A noisy trio exploring sonic dissonances and cross-genre intricacies, navigating between no wave, noise and punk. Expert at dashing out cascades of sound accelerating, rotting and falling apart. In a cultural and social climate reliant on rules and adherence to standards of practice, BRAK stands totemic and atomic.
FFO: Cuntroaches, Load Records, Destruction Unit
|| LOUSE (BE)
Purveyors of the finest cellar-dweller scum rock. A carnival in an open sewer. Whatever septic fluid got in the water in Sweden managed to find its way to the grim English north. The perfect amalgamation of their other projects; Foot Hair and The Shits. Do the wrong thing, and answer the call.
FFO: Brainbombs, Drunk in Hell, not wearing a shirt because it’s soaked in beer
|| TZU NI (TW)
Momentary isolation from the outer world, intense perception of one’s frame of mind & body. A sound artist from Taiwan who recently shifted to the Netherlands. Her work primarily focuses on spatial and sonic installations, field recordings, the acoustics of objects, and computer-generated noise.
FFO: Nurse With Wound, Joe Colley, Jiyoung Wi
|| ASCH (NL)
Familiar faces treading unfamiliar territory.
FFO: Violent Shogun, Kyle Flanagan, Spring of Life
