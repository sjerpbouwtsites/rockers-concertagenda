BLIND AMBITION
punk-hardcore band from Amsterdam that features current and ex members of bands such as Vitamin X, Open Wounds and Betercore. Irina on vocals, Marko on guitar, Nemanja slaying the bass and Geertjan hammering the drums. Their music is inspired by classic ’77 punk style and the early 80’s USHC but then spiced up with some garage, Oi punk and post-punk influences.
