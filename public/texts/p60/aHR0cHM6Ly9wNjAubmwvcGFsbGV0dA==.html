Pallett is gearing up for its 2024 world tour titled 'Ba Man Khial Kon' (Imagine with Me), spanning across Australia, Europe, and Northern America.

Building on the success of its previous tour eight years ago, the band plans to captivate audiences with performances showcasing songs from their latest album 'Nesf ol Nahar' (Prime Meridian), along with their popular hits such as 'Mosallas,' 'Tamam e Natamam,' and 'Naro Baman.'
