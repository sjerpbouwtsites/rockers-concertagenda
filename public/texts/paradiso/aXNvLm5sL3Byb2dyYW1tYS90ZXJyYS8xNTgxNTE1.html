Every Friday, Parallel opens the doors to Terra: a fresh weekly club concept showcasing sounds from the global electronic underground. For this week's edition we have a special line up curated by our resident Unruly Phoenix. She is inviting Nicky Summers, Nyame Cmplx and RVFA to create a night with infectious energy. With a blend of different Afro Electric sounds they take their listeners on a musical journey that reflect the vast creativity of their music.

About: Terra

Welcome to a new home ground in Amsterdam where sounds from the global diaspora collide and mutate with the western electronic sphere, creating new matter united by a percussive, bass-driven cohesion.

Together with a team of residents and weekly guests, Terra showcases a weekly constellation of Afro house, Amapiano, baile funk, batida, gqom, futuristic mutations of dancehall, reggaeton and many more under one roof.
