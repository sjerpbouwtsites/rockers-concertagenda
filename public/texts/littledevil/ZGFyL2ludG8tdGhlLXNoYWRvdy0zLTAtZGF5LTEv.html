We are thrilled to announce Into The Shadow 3.0, our very own homegrown festival celebrating the
almighty Riff! The saga continues, so get ready for an early start of your festivalseason again. This
year three days of doom, sludge, stoner, space, acid, and heavy psychedelic rock. Thursday we ignite
our mission into space with three kick-ass Dutch bands. Friday and Saturday we will be guided
through the Cosmos by an eclectic mix of bands that are way out there

Thursday 7 March 2024

Desert Colossus (NL)

A four piece high energy, fuzzy, heavy stoner rock band that will “blow you off your socks”. One of
their distinctive trademarks is their atypical vocals for the genre. DESERT COLOSSUS is hailing from
Zaandam. For fans of Fu Manchu, Red Fang and all things that are great about desert rock in general.

Bismut (NL)

Three seasoned musicians discovered their chemistry in jamsessions, exploring the boundaries of
instrumental progressive heavy psych ever since. BISMUT is hailing from Nijmegen. They have made
quite an impression on the European scene, with their distinctive sound.

Supersonic Blues (NL)

Three buddies laying down that heavy, fuzzed-out, psyched-up, blues- based rock n’ soul. No
strangers to The Hague’s gritty underground scene, SUPERSONIC BLUES have an unhealthy
obsession with diggin’ out that one obscure 60s/70s rarity. Still, these guys sure enjoy a 21st-century
dystopian boogie! For fans of: Cream, Blue Cheer, Taste, Grand Funk, Sabbath and the Hendrix
Experience. The diehard Roadburner might have enjoyed one of their many appearances.
