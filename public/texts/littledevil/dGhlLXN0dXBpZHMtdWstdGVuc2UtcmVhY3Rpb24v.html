THE STUPIDS

The Stupids formed in Ipswich, Suffolk, England in 1983 after Wolfie and Tommy met through the letters section in Punk Lives magazine. Based on a thriving punk scene in their home town centered around legendary local record shop Parrot Records the Stupids trail blazed a fresh new American style and attitude and revolutionized the UKHC scene. Things would never be the same again.
