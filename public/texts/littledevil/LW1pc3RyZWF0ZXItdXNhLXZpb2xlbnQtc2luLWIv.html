Mean Mistreater hailing from Texas, USA will be hitting Europe to promote the release of their second album “Do or Die” which will be released on February 21st via Dying Victims Productions!
If their debut release may count as an indication of what to expect… Be ready for some hard-hitting, no-nonsense, bad-ass Heavy Metal. This one promises to be ferociously fiery!
Violent Sin from West-Flanders, Belgium will do the honours of supporting Mean Mistreater for this show. With fast paced and utterly tight Speed Metal they’ll crank up the intensity and undoubtedly get you in the exact right spirit!
