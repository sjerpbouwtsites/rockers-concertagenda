After the San Francisco City Trip, the men of Her Majesty travel 600 kilometers south to Los Angeles, the hotspot of classic West Coast pop since 1965. It will be a great evening, featuring wonderful anecdotes, atmospheric visuals, and the timeless music of artists such as The Byrds, Buffalo Springfield, The Doors, and The Beach Boys.
