Steve Von Till, singer and guitarist of the influential band Neurosis, will be coming to De Oosterpoort on Saturday, June 8, with his new album Alone In A World Of Wounds.

On this seventh solo album, he takes a different path: melancholic Americana, drones, and ambient textures are woven together with haunting cello arrangements. The music conveys a deep connection to nature and reflects on loss, intuition, and creativity. Live, Von Till is joined by Dave French (synths, percussion, bass) and either Brent Arnold or Michaela Lilla Bos on cello. Fans of artists like Nick Cave, Talk Talk, and Low will find plenty here to sink into.
