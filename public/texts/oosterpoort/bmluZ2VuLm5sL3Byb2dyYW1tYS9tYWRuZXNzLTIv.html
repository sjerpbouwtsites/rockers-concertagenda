When you say ska, you say Madness, and when you say Madness, you mention the British pop giant with an enormous repertoire that has been rocking festival fields and venues upside down since the 1980s. Of course, the band’s iconic hits like ‘One Step Beyond,’ ‘Our House,’ ‘Baggy Trousers,’ ‘Embarrassment,’ and ‘Night Boat To Cairo’ contribute to this. And that’s just a small selection from their extensive catalog, which now consists of a whopping 16 (!!!) albums. The live shows of ‘The Nutty Boys’ have achieved legendary status, and if your latest accomplishment is named ‘Theatre Of The Absurd Presents C’est La Vie,’ the corresponding tour can only confirm that. On Thursday, September 19, you can experience it for yourself in Groningen, as we present Madness at Martiniplaza!
