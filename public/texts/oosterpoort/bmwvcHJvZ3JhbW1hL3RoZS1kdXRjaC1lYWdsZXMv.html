The Dutch Eagles have been on the road for twenty years now. During this time, the group has given their own twist to big hits and lesser-known songs by The Eagles and other world-famous acts from the seventies. In 2025, the Dutch Eagles will reflect on their greatest moments and musical highlights, featuring classics and occasionally an exciting detour.
