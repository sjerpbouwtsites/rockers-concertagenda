The much-acclaimed Anna Ternheim returns to the essence of making music: raw, honest, and direct. With her sultry, moody voice – steeped in folk, roots, and Scandinavian melancholy – she brings, during the PREMONITIONS Tour, not a polished show, but an evening full of new work in the making.

Anna tests her songs live, on stage, in connection with the audience. She invites you to an evening that feels like a beginning – a musical journey where nothing is set in stone, where there’s room for experimentation and the unexpected.
