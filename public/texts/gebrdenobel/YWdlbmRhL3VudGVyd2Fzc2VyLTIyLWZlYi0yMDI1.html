Good resolution for 2025: To the sharks!

The darkest party in Leiden brings, as you have come to expect from us, a biting line-up with the best alternative electronica, industrial and dark techno.  

We go deep again with a big neo-industrial live show from the French Moaan Exis and drown the rest of the night in a diverse mix of national and international DJs.
