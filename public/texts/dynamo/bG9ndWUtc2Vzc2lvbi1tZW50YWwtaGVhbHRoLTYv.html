Hi you,

Together with Eindhoven in Dialoog and Dynamo Eindhoven, The Brainfreeze Club is introducing; mental health dialogue sessions.

During the dialogue session we will have a conversation with a small group of people and trained conversation guides about different topics in mental health.

We think it is very important to have a small group where we can have a personal and also safe atmosphere. That is why there are only limited places in this session.

Practical info:
When: Wednesday 14th of May ’25 at 19:00 until 21.30
Where: Dynamo Eindhoven – ROOM 3
Get your FREE tickets here

We have four steps in the dialogue:

1. Getting acquainted

2. Sharing personal positive experiences

3. Dreaming

4. Doing.

Questions? Let us know by email or Insta DM.
