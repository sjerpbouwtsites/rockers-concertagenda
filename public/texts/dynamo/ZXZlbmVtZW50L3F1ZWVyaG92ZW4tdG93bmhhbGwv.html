Queerhoven Townhall is a public community meeting. You can pitch ideas, ask questions, give feedback or just enjoy the time with each other. We are eager to help our wider community connect and organize together with more agency. The core team will focus on coordination and resource distribution, as one central hub, so everyone is able to participate in whatever way they can.
