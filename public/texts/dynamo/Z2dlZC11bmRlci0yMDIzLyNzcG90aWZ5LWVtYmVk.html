Helaas heeft Dragged Under besloten om hun tour te annuleren. De show in Dynamo die gepland stond voor 23 april gaat dan ook niet door. Kaartkopers ontvangen een mail vanuit Ticketmaster over de terugstorting van het aankoopbedrag.
 
Statement band:
 
Hey friends, we wanted to take a second to update you on where we’re at lately. We’ve been quiet and it’s for good reason. We’ve been evaluating what the future of the band looks like, and while we still plan on releasing music this year, (which is already in the works) we are in dire need of a break from full time touring. We will be taking some time off to focus on raising our growing families, pursuing other passions and just taking a break from the rigorous schedule that we’ve been on for the past few years.
 
How long this will last, we’re not sure. We appreciate your patience, understanding and the continued support of our music. Apologies to anyone who was looking forward to seeing us in EU this spring.
