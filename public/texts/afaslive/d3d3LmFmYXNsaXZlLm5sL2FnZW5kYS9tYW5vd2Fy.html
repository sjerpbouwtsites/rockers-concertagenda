MANOWAR’s “The Blood Of Our Enemies Tour 2025” rages onward with the band’s long-awaited return to Amsterdam. The ONLY show in the Netherlands will take place on January 27, 2025, at Amsterdam’s AFAS Live.

The wild heavy metal fortress of Amsterdam is the perfect setting to host this metal onslaught for MANOWAR’s Dutch Army of Immortals, with the band performing hits from their milestone albums "Hail to England" and "Sign of the Hammer"—along with other iconic anthems that crowned them the Kings of Metal!

“It’s going to be a historic night true metal fans will want to be a part of!” MANOWAR bassist Joey De Maio said. “This concert is for the Dutch fans who have never let us down and never will! They are the loudest, wildest, heaviest metal fans in the whole country!”

About MANOWAR
In 1980 a new band exploded on the metal scene that would write metal history, inventing a unique genre, aptly named Power Metal and changing the lives of millions since; with songs that have become battle hymns and a sound like no other. Capturing audiences around the globe with their message of hope and perseverance and a passion and determination that has defied any obstacles since. Their name: MANOWAR!

With unfaltering courage to always do what they believe in, this band has weathered even the most unconquerable challenges. For over four decades, MANOWAR, the Kings Of Metal as their fans crowned them, have been giving their fans what they love them for: the massive, powerful soundtrack of their lives, songs that celebrate the audacity of the individual and the spirit of the community, and qualities such as loyalty, honesty, and integrity.

Numerous gold and platinum records and more than 40 tours including headlining performances between 10,000 and 50,000 sold-out seats testify to their status in the metal universe.
MANOWAR’s worldwide loyal fan base is also showcased in their live events: For the band’s first self-organized Magic Circle Festival, fans traveled from more than 40 nations, and at any MANOWAR concert a sea of flags document the distances their worldwide followers travel to watch their favorite band and unite with their fellow Manowarriors. As a tribute to this devoted international following, MANOWAR’s release “Thunder In The Sky” achieved another first in Heavy Metal history by featuring the power ballad, “Father” in 18 different languages.

In 2022 the band surprised their fans with their unique take on Homer’s Odyssey, including the song “Immortal” and narrations in Ancient Greek by iconic Greek actor Kostas Kazakos and his son Konstantinos Kazakos.

For 2024 MANOWAR announced the re-recording of the 1984 album “Sign Of The Hammer”, that shall be released as "The Sign Of The Hammer MMXXIV", promising “the same brutality from the first release, combined with the inspiration you have all provided over the years, amplified by the power modern technology offers. The 2024 release will blast the ground and melt your faces more intensely and louder than ever, delivering maximum metal!”

MANOWAR also announced the release a brand-new studio album with fresh tracks in 2025 that “will crush the enemies of metal and unite our Manowarriors the world over, under the banner of True Metal; stronger than ever!” according to the band.

Celebrating the 45th anniversary of their foundation, MANOWAR will set out on the "Blood of Our Enemies Tour 2025,” celebrating "Hail To England" and "The Sign Of The Hammer MMXXIV" albums respectively, and each evening will showcase additional fan favorites from MANOWAR’s vast career, unleashed in a brand new stage production!
