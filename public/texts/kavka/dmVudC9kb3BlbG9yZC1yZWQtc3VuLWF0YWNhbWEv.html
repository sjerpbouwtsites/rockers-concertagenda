Sonic Excess presents

Hailing from Poland and relentlessly practicing dark arts and riff worship ever since their conception in 2010, Dopelord return to Antwerp to burn amps, infiltrate minds and devour all the herbs and Duvels that cross their way on and off the stage. Living up to their name, over the years Dopelord have established a rock-solid sense of self-identity by being prolific, unwavering and most importantly – fully committed to the ways and wisdom passed on by God himself – Tony Iommi. Come to the sabbath!
