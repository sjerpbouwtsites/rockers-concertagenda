Dopelord: Hailing from Poland, Dopelord brings a heavy blend of stoner and doom metal, characterized by crushing riffs, dense atmospheres, and hypnotic rhythms. Their music delves into the realms of cosmic psychedelia while maintaining a raw and gritty edge, earning them a devoted following in the underground metal scene.

Red Sun Atacama: Red Sun Atacama is a dynamic rock band known for their electrifying fusion of desert rock, blues, and psychedelia. Originating from Chile, their sound captures the essence of vast desert landscapes and primal energy. With soulful vocals, blistering guitar solos, and infectious grooves, Red Sun Atacama delivers a sonic journey that is both exhilarating and introspective.
