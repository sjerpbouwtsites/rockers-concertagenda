Dying Fetus is an American death metal band formed in 1991 in Upper Marlboro, Maryland. Known for their technical prowess, brutal sound, and politically charged lyrics, the band has established a significant presence within the extreme metal scene. Over the years, Dying Fetus has become renowned for their intricate guitar work, complex song structures, and relentless live performances.
