ANNISOKAY is returning to European stages with the largest headline tour in the band’s
history, continuing their acclaimed Abyss Tour and presenting Abyss Pt. II.
After playing over 20 shows across the continent in 2023—many of them sold out—and
supporting Within Temptation as a main support in arenas throughout 2024, the band is
now taking the next step: 30 shows in 14 countries across all over Europe !
