Fit for an Autopsy: Fit for an Autopsy is a metal band known for their blend of deathcore and hardcore elements, delivering powerful and introspective music. Formed in 2008, their sound is characterized by intense riffs, crushing breakdowns, and poignant lyrics that often explore themes of existentialism, societal decay, and personal struggle.

Sylosis: Sylosis is a British metal band recognized for their technical prowess and melodic sensibilities. Established in 2000, they have crafted a unique sound that merges elements of thrash, death, and progressive metal. With intricate guitar work, dynamic rhythms, and thought-provoking lyrics, Sylosis creates music that is both complex and captivating, appealing to fans of diverse metal subgenres.

Darkest Hour: Darkest Hour is a pioneering force in the American metalcore scene, renowned for their ferocious energy and relentless aggression. Formed in 1995, they have consistently pushed the boundaries of the genre with their blend of melodic hooks, blistering riffs, and politically charged lyrics. With a career spanning over two decades, Darkest Hour continues to inspire audiences with their passionate and incendiary performances.

Heriot: Heriot is a rising metalcore band hailing from the UK, known for their explosive sound and emotive songwriting. Formed in recent years, they have quickly made a name for themselves with their dynamic compositions, combining punishing breakdowns with atmospheric melodies. With introspective lyrics that touch on themes of personal growth and resilience, Heriot offers a fresh perspective within the contemporary metalcore landscape.
