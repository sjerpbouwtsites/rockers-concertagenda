VUKOVI are hitting the road in 2025 with their MY GOD HAS GOT A GUN TOUR, and they’re bringing the energy to Zappa, Antwerpen on February 20, 2025! After tearing up the stage at Graspop Metal Meeting earlier this summer, the band is ready to deliver another unforgettable performance. Supporting them are the darkly intense unpeople and rising alt-pop force Artio.

Their upcoming album, MY GOD HAS GOT A GUN, drops on January 24th, capturing the raw spirit of their time in Texas.

This night promises powerful performances and unforgettable vibes – don’t miss out!
