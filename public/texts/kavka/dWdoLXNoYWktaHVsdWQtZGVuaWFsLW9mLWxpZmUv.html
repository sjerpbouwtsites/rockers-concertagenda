DARKEST HOUR

Darkest Hour is a metalcore band from Washington, D.C., formed in 1995. Known for blending melodic death metal with hardcore punk, they deliver aggressive, fast-paced music with politically and emotionally charged lyrics. Albums like Undoing Ruin (2005) and Deliver Us (2007) solidified their place in the modern metalcore scene.

BLEEDING THROUGH

Bleeding Through is a metalcore band from Orange County, California, formed in 1999. They mix elements of black metal, death metal, and hardcore, creating a powerful and melodic sound. Their breakthrough album This Is Love, This Is Murderous (2003) helped define the early 2000s American metalcore movement.

SHAI HULUD

Shai Hulud is a seminal hardcore band from Florida, formed in 1995. Known for their thought-provoking lyrics and technical musicianship, they are pioneers of melodic hardcore and early metalcore. Their debut Hearts Once Nourished with Hope and Compassion (1997) is considered a classic in the genre.
