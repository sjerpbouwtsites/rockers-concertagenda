Our Last Night

On 24 September, the American post-hardcore band Our Last Night is coming to Zappa, Antwerp! Formed in 2004 in Hollis, New Hampshire, this dynamic post-hardcore band consists of Trevor and Matthew Wentworth and Timothy Molloy. The band quickly rose to prominence through innovative covers of popular songs on platforms like YouTube, gaining a worldwide following. Known for blending post-hardcore, alternative rock, and metalcore, their original music, including albums like “The Ghosts Among Us” and “Younger Dreams,” showcases powerful vocals, intricate guitar work, and emotionally charged lyrics. With a commitment to pushing musical boundaries and constant engagement with fans, Our Last Night remains an influential presence in the contemporary rock scene
