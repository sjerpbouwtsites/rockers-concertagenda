Hantrax + Shaka Shams + monokimono + STICE + Stayinbedpropaganda

The ONWEER festival is all about music with a dark feel and vibe, regardless the genre or style. Come dance, sing along, bang your head, cry a tear and discover your new favorite artist.

Take a look at your favorite Spotify playlist. Whether it’s your ‘Hit the gym’ or ‘Brat girl summer’ playlist, chances are there’s five different genres and ten different styles in there. But it works, your playlist perfectly fits your mood. That’s exactly how ONWEER is put together: he night goes from dark beats to heavy guitars and from moody soundscapes to ominous vocals. Ready for a dark night out?
