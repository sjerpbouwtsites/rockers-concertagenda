Sonic Excess presents

SPEED is a hardcore band known for their aggressive sound and focus on identity and community. They released a demo in 2019 and partnered with Flatspot Records for a 2020 EP. Their upcoming EP, *Gang Called Speed*, emphasizes self-identity and defiance. Influenced by Biohazard and Trapped Under Ice, they aim to put Australian hardcore on the map. Despite limited live shows, SPEED is set for a breakthrough year with more performances planned, including a debut at Sound and Fury in Los Angeles.
