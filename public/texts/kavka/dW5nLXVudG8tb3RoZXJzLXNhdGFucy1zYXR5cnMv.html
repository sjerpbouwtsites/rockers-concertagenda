Green Lung is a British heavy rock band known for their blend of doom metal, psychedelic rock, and occult themes. Their music often features dark, mystical lyrics with a vintage, 70s-inspired sound, combining heavy riffs, melodic hooks, and a strong sense of atmosphere.
