From Fall to Spring is a rising revelation in the nu-metal scene. This German band made waves during the Eurovision Song Contest pre-selection, and while they didn’t make it to the finals, they quickly built a passionate fanbase. Known for their explosive live shows, they’ve rocked major festivals and big-name stages across Germany.

Blending metal, electronic elements, pop, and rap with sharp precision, their sound echoes influences like Linkin Park, Limp Bizkit, and Electric Callboy. Their debut album Rise is a bold, catchy statement full of energy and emotion. Active on TikTok and full of stage presence, From Fall to Spring brings pure adrenaline—get ready to rock and rave.

SPECIAL GUEST: CYAN KICKS

Cyan Kicks is a Helsinki-based electronic modern rock band known for their explosive sound, emotional depth, and sharp production. Blending influences from PVRIS and CHVRCHES with Nordic melancholy, they deliver soaring vocals, tight rhythms, and cinematic electronic textures.

After a string of singles, their 2019 debut album I Don’t Love You earned them an Emma Award nomination for Best Rock Album. They followed up with the bold EP Not Your Kind and entered a new era with Invincible and their Eurovision 2022 entry Hurricane. With a second full-length album in sight, Cyan Kicks continues to push modern rock forward.

SUPPORT: LETTERS SENT HOME

Letters Sent Home fuse the emotional pull of early 2000s alternative rock with powerful melodies and raw honesty. The Lower Saxony-based band has quickly built a loyal fanbase and made a strong debut with their anthemic, high-energy sound; perfect for life’s highs and lows. Signed to Sharptone Records, they’re just as unforgettable live as they are on record.
