Skinny Lister
Hailing from the UK, Skinny Lister are a rowdy folk-punk outfit known for their high-energy live shows, foot-stomping anthems, and infectious camaraderie. Blending traditional folk instrumentation with a punk spirit, the band has built a devoted fanbase across Europe and North America. With a catalog that ranges from sea shanties to full-blown rockers, Skinny Lister are a true festival favorite, bringing a party wherever they play.

Vandoliers
Support comes from Vandoliers, a fiery six-piece from Dallas, Texas, who fuse punk rock urgency with rootsy Americana swagger. With their genre-blending sound and heartfelt storytelling, Vandoliers have carved out a unique space in the alt-country scene. Expect grit, twang, and plenty of passion from this hard-touring band that wears its heart—and its Texas roots—on its sleeve.
