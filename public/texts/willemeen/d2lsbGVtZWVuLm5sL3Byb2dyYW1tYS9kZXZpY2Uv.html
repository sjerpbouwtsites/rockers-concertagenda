Device is a collective consisting of DJs, audiovisual artists and designers.

After experimenting with virtual events, audiovisual installations and several one-off hostings and events, we are now diving head first into club nights.

We will go beyond house and techno, and land in the realm of forward thinking club music. To aid us in that quest we have invited a true sonic pioneer: French II. Drawing from a wide range of influences, his functional and murky, breaks- and percussion-heavy sound especially depicts French II's deep-rooted ties to UK bass music.

French II is supported by Device residents Phinny, Five Suns, T9 and Veld.

You've seen Device before in collaborations with local events like Forms and Sauce.
