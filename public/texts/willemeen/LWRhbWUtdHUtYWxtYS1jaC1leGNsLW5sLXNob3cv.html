WE COME ALIVE TOUR 2025
GOTHMINISTER embarks on a new European Tour in March 2025 to promote the critically acclaimed and latest album "Pandemonium II: The Battle of The Underworlds".

GOTHMINISTER has been described as one of the most iconic gothic industrial artists in the scene today. GOTHMINISTER started up in 1999 and has toured more than 40 countries and have recieved great reviews for fantastic and captivating theatrical live shows.

"Pandemonium" was voted "Dark Album of the Year" and hit no.1 on Deutsche Alternative Charts. "Pandemonium II..." included the hit "We Come Alive" which was performed by GOTHMINISTER live for more than 2 million TV-viewers in the national Eurovision Song Contest selections for Norway.

Get ready for a great night with 4 bands giving you the best they've got. coming ALIVE on stage!
