Gabber Resistance strongly rises again with another edition. The hardcore forces are on the move one more time.

This time we bring you a legend from Scotland: DJ Davie Forbes released tracks on legendary labels such as: Twisted Vinyl, Evolution Records, Babyboom Records, Screwdriver Records, Rave Records, Bass Generator Records and many more.

We will do our best to take you back to the golden era of hardcore. No new style, pure oldschool hardcore from start to finish. Hakkeh all night long! Our dedicated artists will show their skills on the decks in this vinyl-only event. We will bring back hardcore the way it was, on VINYL!

As always, recording or taking pictures will NOT be allowed. Back in the '90s, mobile phones with cameras didn't exist. People were more connected to the music. That is why at Gabber Resistance, we would like to recreate that vibe. Stop recording, start dancing! We kindly ask everyone to obey this rule in order to contribute to the unique '90s vibe.

Make sure to grab your ticket as soon as possible as last time the event was sold out around one week before. If this happens, there will be no door sale.

STOP FILMING, START DANCING!
Be prepared for a night of dancefloor madness... get ready for some MINDLESS MAYHEM!!

The event will be from 23:55-06:00. See you there gabbers!
