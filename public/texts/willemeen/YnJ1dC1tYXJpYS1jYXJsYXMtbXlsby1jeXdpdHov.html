Maria Carlas & Mylo Cywitz (IT/NL, Arnhem/Amsterdam)

Maria Carlas brings her operatic voice, impulsive key work and synth ballads to Mylo Cywitz classical pop industrial compositions. Their live show includes a costumed show, a candle lit music stand and accompanying visuals as they wail on obscure minisynths, a glockenspiel, a mandolin, bass guitar and a recorder.

Maria Carlas (IT) and Mylo Cywitz (NL) started in 2017 performing shows together. They have performed all over Europe separately as solo artists and in groups. Together they have performed in Portugal, Bulgaria, Germany and the Netherlands.

Salon Brut (Arnhem)
Gemma meulendijks: Cello, launchpad, vocal.
Quinten Dierick: Synths, tape, percussion.

We play improvisation music since 2020. We use not only our musical instruments but deploy foremost the room , the space and circumstances in general. The music we bring ranges from the most primitive and elementary, to the very theatrical and back. We roughly set up and there the adventure starts. These recordings are copies of the audiocassettes we use for recording our sessions. No edits of effects are applied. Have fun listening! Quinten & Gemma
