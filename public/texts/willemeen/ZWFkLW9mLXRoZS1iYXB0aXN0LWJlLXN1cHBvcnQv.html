Arnhem Noise Attack is BACK!
After a long hiatus Arnhem Noise Attack is back! And very happy to offer your eardrums some of the best bands that the underground DIY scene has to offer again!

Final Dose
Black metal-infused hardcore punk from London.
Final Dose, like a haunting specter, arose during the 2020 lockdown as a solitary entity. Since then, they've transformed into a 4-piece coven, channeling the chaotic energies of black metal-tinged hardcore punk. Their incantations have summoned "Dark Places" and the mesmerizing "World Prisoner" in 2022. Last fall, they crossed the Atlantic to the United States, before returning to European soil in 2024 to unleash the terror that resides within their debut LP, "Void Inside."

Head Of The Baptist
HOTB is a Black infested Death/Crust band from Belgium.
The name Head of the Baptist may conjure images of torch-bearing, satanic Black Metal worshippers atop snow-covered peaks. However, this band is an enigmatic fusion of heavy genres. Weaving together crushing Black and Death metal influences, while embracing the fistful rage of D-Beat/Crust and the chaos of Grindcore.
Next to their previous demo release in 2018, their fresh EP "The Pyre Of Equivalence" was released digitally earlier this year and will be available physically before the tour.
