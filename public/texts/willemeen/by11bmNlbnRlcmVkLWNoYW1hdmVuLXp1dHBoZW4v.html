LOTHARIO
Lothario is the visceral, razor-edged solo project of Melbourne's Annaliese Redlich, who creates punk anthems for the bored, horny, and reckless. Conceived in her bedroom in November 2022, Lothario's music is a raw outpouring of late-night hedonism, encapsulating themes of sex, conflict, and rebellion. Redlich's songwriting is a cathartic exploration of betrayal, booze, boyfriends, and bullies - offering a momentary escape from self-doubt and vulnerability. Her music challenges listeners to confront their demons and seize their power.

Lothario's inception was marked by a lo-fi immediacy, with Redlich recording all the instruments, except drums, in her living room. The result is an urgent, desperate menace that resonates through her lyrics and soundscapes. The songs are love letters to losers, filled with an unfiltered intensity that invites listeners to rage until daybreak.

Lothario quickly gained traction, selling out singles through labels such as Goodbye Boozy, Under The Gun, and Wintergarden. Following a successful 23-date USA tour in October and November 2023, Lothario is set to conquer Europe in October and November 2024, booked by Wildwax. Hitting your ears next is the full length 13 track album "Hogtied" available in September 2024. Lothario will perform as a full band!

Lothario is more than just a solo project; it's a rebellion against the mundane, a sonic embodiment of late-night debauchery, and a testament to the power of Annaliese’s unyielding spirit.

UNCENTERED
Uncentered is a alternative rockband from Arnhem/Zutphen. The band was formed in early 2024. Uncentered plays a mix of hard rock and punk with pop influences. "We distinguish ourself through our powerful and energetic stage presence and our memorable and catchy hooks"

All the members of the group grew up listening to the music our parents played for us in the car, from Nirvana to Muse and from Dream Theater to The Police.

We gained a love/passion for Rock and Roll from a young age, it is with this passion we play our hearts out at every gig!
