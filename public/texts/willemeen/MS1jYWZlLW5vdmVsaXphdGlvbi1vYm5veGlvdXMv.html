Utilize The Remains
Utilize The Remains are a 4-Piece Brutal Slamming Death Metal Band, proudly cultivated in New Zealand. Formed in 2021, they burst onto their local scene with Visceral Intent. Laced with Heavy Sledgehammer Slams and Apocalyptic themes, their live performance is absolutely crushing! Having shared the stage with bands such as Blood Incantation, Disentomb and Organectomy, Utilize The Remains are well on the way to carving their own path in the underground. They released a 3-track demo in 2021, recently followed up by their debut full-length album: “Psychotic Abyss”. Both through Slam Worldwide. Utilize The Remains will be supporting long standing death metal titans ‘Obituary’ along with Australian hard-hitters ‘Psycroptic’ on their tour across New Zealand in January 2024. With no signs of stopping there, UTR will continue to obliterate next year and prepare themselves for the road to OBSCENE EXTREME!!!
