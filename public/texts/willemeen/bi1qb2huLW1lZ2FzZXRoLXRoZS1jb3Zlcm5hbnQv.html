DEAD HEAD
Dead Head is a Dutch thrash metal band from the city Kampen. The band was formed in 1989 by the four original members Tom van Dijk (bass, vocals), Robbie Woning (guitar), Ronnie van der Wey (guitar), and Hans Spijker (drums). Their mission was to become the most intensive and brutal thrash metal band Europe ever had seen. They were influenced by classic thrash metal bands like Dark Angel, Kreator and Sadus.

They released their debut album in 1991, and have released seven studio albums and one 10 inch. Their latest work is the EP via Hammerheart Records named Shadow of Souls made in 2024. Go check it out!

TEN TON JOHN
Ten Ton John is a metal band from the South Holland region and was founded at the end of 2017. The band alternates pumping grooving drum and guitar parts with sharp thrashy riffs and grunts with catchy vocals and can be compared to old Machine Head and Gojira.

THE COVERNANT (tribute)
The Covernant has been throwing a party on every stage since 2009. The band plays a set full of hard rock and metal classics, which everyone is already roaring along to after a few beers. Think of old hits from bands like Slayer, Metallica, Sepultura, Iron Maiden, Judas Priest, Anthrax, Machine Head, Motörhead, AC/DC, Ozzy Osbourne and Pantera. New classics from bands such as Steel Panther, Airbourne and Gojira are also on the ever-growing set list. The five have now completed more than 135 shows and have therefore grown into a tight party machine.
