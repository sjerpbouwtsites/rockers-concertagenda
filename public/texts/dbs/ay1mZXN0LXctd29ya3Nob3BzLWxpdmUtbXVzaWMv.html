An entire day dedicated to the powers of DIY Riot Grrrl Punk with workshops and blistering live music!
Starting at noon, The RIOT HOUSE will host the workshops ‘Start Your Own Band In One Day’ and ‘Skate against Patriarchy’. Workshops are free for ticket holders and have limited capacity. Register at jessica@theriothouse.nl

At 6 pm participants of the Start Your Own Band workshop will do a short performance followed by a host of bands including:
MARIA ISKARIOT – The Joan of Arc of Punk and winners of Humo’s Rock Rally 2024. Rising stars yet fiercely uncompromising. A whirlwind on stage.
PERIOT – Arnhem’s own Riot Grrls outfit and instigators of the infamous Kutfeest. Finally on the dB’s stage ready to take you by surprise
PROBLEM PATTERS – A wee queer DIY feminist Punk band from Ulster. Invited to open up for the likes of Le Tigre and Henry Rollins. Righteous anger has rarely sounded so engaging.
CONSENSUAL PEGGY – Utrecht’s own conceptual punk innovators with tremendous improvisational talent and a general dislike to public anticipation.
THE DOLLY ADLERS – Loud and legendary fuck-wave punk band led by powerhouse Virginia Adler. Ready to fuck shit up in the pit.
