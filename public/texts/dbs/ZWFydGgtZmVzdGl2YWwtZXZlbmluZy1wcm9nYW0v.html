The 5th edition of Black Earth Festival focuses on resilient communities through the doomest of times.
A carefully selected line up of international artists will investigate rituals of proximity, loudness, anger and hope with their music and performances.
On Saturday 16th at dB’s, a maelstrom of rifs, hypnotic chants and crepuscular beats will weave unexpected music textures for us all to join, enjoy and dance on.
Line-up:
 
  • Lili Refrain – IT ( Multi instrumental ritual folk – exclusive Dutch show)
  • Dalila Kayros – IT ( musical creature beyond genres)
  • Designer Violence – NL ( Electro noise-punk)
  • Ismena Band – NL (Melancholic dark folk)
  • Dj Maria aka Nachmerrie (dark wave- experimental)
