START YOUR PUNK BAND IN 1 DAY

 

Wanna start a punk band but don’t know where to start?

The Riot House offers a series of workshops with the goal to bring diversity and inclusivity in DIY music within a safer and supportive environment for people new to playing music.

We highly support and encourage women, persons of colour, lgbtqia+, neurodiverse, disabled persons to join.

People of any musical experience can attend our workshops, though they are largely aimed at beginners. You will learn to play an instrument (guitar, bass, drums, keyboards, or vocals), form a band, write a song, and perform your song at the end of the day as the opening act for the festival.

You do not have to bring your own instrument, although you are welcome to do so. We will provide instruments and equipment during the workshop. In your registration, you will be asked to pick two different instruments you would be interested in playing in your band. The choices are bass, drums, guitar, keyboards, or vocals.

Our coaches will help you to explore musical improvisation and creative freedom in a punk, DIY and empowering way and guide you through the process to ultimately play your song on stage at the end of the day.

Cost to attend our workshop is free but you are welcome to donate a pay what you want fee to support our organisation and coaches.

You’ll supply the PUNK attitude, we’ll supply the rest!
