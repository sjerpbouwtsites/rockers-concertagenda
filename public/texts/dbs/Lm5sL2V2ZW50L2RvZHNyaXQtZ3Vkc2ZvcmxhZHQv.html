Hailing from the deep forests of Borlänge, and decaying concrete of Amsterdam comes DÖDSRIT. Formed in 2017 as a one-man project, but now in full force as a full-band, they merge soaring blackened onslaught, treacherous unsetting calm drones and a blizzard of D-beat infused Black Metal into a violently crashing maelstrom of melancholic and soultearing cacophony.

Most recent release Nocturnal Will is a true masterpiece which will leave you grasping for air and begging for more.

Unrivalled !
