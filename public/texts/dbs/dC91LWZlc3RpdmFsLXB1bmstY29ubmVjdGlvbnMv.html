PUNK CONNECTIONS: music, art & activism through DiY ideology 

If you’d like to emerge yourself in the young and old scene of punk in Utrecht, on the 9th of November you are welcome to visit the free PUNK CONNECTIONS festival in dB’s. Besides from the bands you can see, you can dive into photography, interviews, short video’s and punk vinyls.

If you have doubts about starting a (punk) band, or about playing an instrument this is your moment. We’ll provide a rehearsal room for people to connect and start their band, experience does not matter at all. It’s all about connections.
