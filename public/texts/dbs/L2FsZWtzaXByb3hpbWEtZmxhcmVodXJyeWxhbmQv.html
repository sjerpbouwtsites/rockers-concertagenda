ALEKSI

Aleksi creates inspiring unifying performances, blending the drive of indie rock with the culture of singers-songwriters. He performs songs in various languages, including English, Russian and Georgian, reflecting on his status as a perpetual “outsider” in search of identity. His music is an atmospheric and hypnotic journey for those who have lost their homeland and fear rejection.

In his project Oorzicht (earsight)  Aleksi Integrates video art from anti-war artists and interviews, conveying  a never ending actuality of restating the humanitarian values. His work is a call to remember that we all seek connection and understanding in anti-militarisation, regardless of our backgrounds. Aleksis’ “Oorzicht” is also his graduation concert from the conservatory of Utrecht.

PROXIMA FLARE

Proxima Flare is a metal band from Amsterdam and Haarlem, mixing progressive and alternative influences. They make loud guitar music where the drums lead the way and the bass actually matters. Clean vocals tie it all together, giving the band a sound of its own. They’ve released two EPs and are currently working on new music. Their second EP reached over 10,000 streams in the first three months! Over the years, they’ve played plenty of shows across the Netherlands, including opening for Ibuiltthesky, and they’ve also taken their music abroad with a show in Germany.

HURRYLAND

Get ready to get your mind twisted and feelings modulated with the jazz influenced progressive metal band Hurryland! Hurryland blends the heaviness of metal, the improvisation of jazz and the soundscapes of ambience with one simple goal: To have you experience that feeling you only get when you listen to your favorite song. By drawing inspiration from the melodies of Plini, the rhythms of Animals as Leaders and the Djazzy sound of Tigran Hamasyan they manage to create their own unique style for Hurryland. On the 30th of June they play their energetic liveshow in dBs as part of frontman Harry Lunds graduation concert from the conservatory of Utrecht.
