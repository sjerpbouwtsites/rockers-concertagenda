De jaarlijkse Popronde gaat 24 oktober helemaal loos.

20:00 – HALF CAB

HALF CAB is an alternative punkrock band from The Hague consisting of 4 best friends. Since their self-released debut album ‘I’d Rather Be Dead Than 40’ the band has played a ton of shows in and outside of The Netherlands. 2024 marks the year of new releases, starting off with their new single ‘Rearview’ of their eponymous EP dropping in May.

 

21:15 – PARKER FANS

Parker Fans are an Amsterdam indie boogie three-way bromance, made for the later slots in the stickier clubs. Frontman Kick Kluiving lays out his deceptively intricate urban mini-tragedies (and comedies) over a deceptively simple foundation of drum samples, bass guitar and synthesizer. Did we mention you can dance to them? It’s very easy in fact!

 

22:30 – SONIC WHIP

Dansende rock riffs die op sleepketting worden genomen door een dreunende en lucht verschuivende ritmesectie. Dit jonge trio neemt je aan je oorlel mee en zal met hun strakke rocksongs je niet ongeschonden laten. Ervaar de supersonische zweepslag zelf. Je hebt het nodig.
